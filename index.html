ABOUT ME

Oyindamola Nerfatiti Aladesuyi is an award wining Nigerian model, actor, fashion designer, artist content creator and humanitarian, Nerfatiti was born in Ekiti state Nigeria to Mr Bayo and Mrs Mercilina Aladesuyi, a graduate of English and literary studies from federal university Oye Ekiti, she worn several awards in her primary school days as the best female painter also represented her school in inter states art competitions. She started her modeling career in 2012, also contested for Nigeria Next Super Model in 2015, she won Miss Bikini Nigeria in 2016 and has walked the runway for top Nigerian designers and featured in top Nigerian music videos with Nigerian top artistes. She is also part of the Art For Change Africa NGO, an organization that promotes the works of young talented less privileged teenagers and youths from the slum
